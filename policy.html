Privacy Policy

    Your Privacy

  • GETFOOD is committed to protecting your privacy. Any information we collect and store about you will only be used lawfully. GETFOOD will not willfully disclose any confidential information without your prior permission, and your details will not be passed onto any third party without your consent.


    Use of Information: Order Processing: We use the provided personal information to process and deliver your food orders accurately and efficiently.


    Account Management: Your account information is used for authentication, account management, and communication regarding your orders or account-related matters. Improving Services: We may analyze usage data to improve our website's functionality, content, and services.


    Data Security: Encryption: We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Access Control: Access to your personal information is limited to authorized personnel only.


    Sharing of Information: Third-Party Partners: We may share your personal information with trusted third-party partners (e.g., restaurants, delivery providers) to fulfill your orders and deliver services effectively. Legal Compliance: We may share your information if required by law or to protect our legal rights, users, or the public.
